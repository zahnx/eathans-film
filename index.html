About

Greetings and welcome to Eathan's Film and Photography, where visual stories come to life! I am a passionate visual artist specializing in crafting unforgettable moments and weaving captivating narratives through photography and videography, based in the heart of Lemery, Batangas. With a keen eye for detail and a deep love for storytelling, I thrive on transforming ordinary moments into extraordinary memories. My mission is to capture fleeting smiles and encapsulate the essence of your special events, creating timeless and breathtaking visuals that you'll cherish forever. Whether it's an intimate occasion or a grand celebration, I am committed to capturing the magic in every moment. From candid snapshots to cinematic masterpieces, let's collaborate and bring your vision to life! Join me on this artistic journey as we explore the endless possibilities of photography and videography together. Get in touch via email or drop a message here. I can't wait to create stunning, glamorous moments with you! ✨
